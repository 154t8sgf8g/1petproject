Popular solar panel manufacturers

The most widespread in Russia are the products of Chinese manufacturers, due to their relative cheapness compared to products manufactured in other countries. For example, solar panels from China are almost half the price of German ones.

Most often on the shelves are the products of Yingli Green Energy and Suntech Power Co. Also popular are the panels of HiminSolar (China). Their solar panels produce electricity even in rainy weather.

The production of solar panels is established and the domestic manufacturer. These are engaged in these companies:
LLC "Hevel" in Novocheboksarsk;
"Telecom-TV" in Zelenograd;
"Sun Shines" (LLC "Autonomous Lighting Systems") in Moscow;
JSC "Ryazan Plant of Metal-Ceramic Devices";
CJSC "Termotron-Zavod" and others.

In terms of cost, you can always find a suitable option. For example, in Moscow on solar panels for the house the cost will vary from 21 000 to 2 000 000 rubles. The cost depends on the configuration and power of the devices.
