The principle of operation of solar power plant at home

A solar power plant is a system consisting of panels, an inverter, a battery and a controller.
The solar panel transforms radiant energy into electricity (as mentioned above).
The direct current goes to the controller, which distributes the current to consumers (such as a computer or lights).
The inverter converts the direct current to alternating current and powers most electrical household appliances.
Energy is stored in a battery that can be consumed during the hours of darkness.
