An experienced realtor will find the best options

The buyer in the process can relax and quietly explore apartments suitable for their needs. The real estate agent will also take care of: Checking the legal cleanliness of the property the client is interested in.
Control of the prepayment.
All documents necessary for the execution of the contract of sale.
Organization of the transaction. This may even include the transfer of money from the buyer to the seller in comfortable and safe conditions, drawing up and signing documents, state registration.
Even after signing the contract realtor does not leave the client. At his request, the specialist can additionally supervise the transfer of keys, utility receipts, registration in the new apartment and other important moments.

The agent's primary concern is the comfort and convenience of the client.

Even if the seller or buyer is completely ignorant of the law, the realtor will help them to conduct the transaction in accordance with all the norms of current legislation.
In order for a specialist to undertake all of the above tasks, he or she will need high-quality professional training, knowledge and experience.
And also - communication skills, strength, time and nerves.
